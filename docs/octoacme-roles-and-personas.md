# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX/UI Designer

### Role Summary
UX/UI Designers design user flows and interfaces, ensuring usability, accessibility, and a positive user experience. They bridge the gap between user needs and technical implementation.

### Responsibilities
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and design specifications
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Advise on accessibility standards and user-centric best practices
- Collaborate with Product Managers and Developers on requirements and design handoff
- Ensure consistency across the product through design systems

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible user experiences
- Reduce user friction and improve satisfaction metrics
- Maintain design quality and consistency

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and critiques with the team
- Prototype walkthroughs and usability test findings
- Collaboration sessions during feature discovery
- Design specs and handoff documentation

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Product Managers**: Collaborate during discovery to understand user needs and define requirements
- **Developers**: Work together on implementation feasibility, provide design specs, and review final implementations
- **Project Managers**: Participate in planning sessions, provide design effort estimates
- **QA/Testing**: Partner to ensure acceptance criteria include usability and accessibility checks

---

## QA Engineer / Tester

### Role Summary
QA Engineers ensure product quality and release readiness through comprehensive testing and quality assurance practices. They validate that features meet acceptance criteria and identify issues before reaching customers.

### Responsibilities
- Define and execute test plans and test cases
- Perform manual and automated testing (functional, regression, integration, end-to-end)
- File issues, verify fixes, and track quality metrics
- Manage regression and acceptance testing
- Advocate for quality standards and testability

### Goals
- Ensure high product quality and minimal defects in production
- Improve test coverage and automation
- Reduce time to identify and resolve issues

### Typical Communication
- Test reports and quality status updates
- Bug reports and verification notes
- Testing strategy documentation
- Participation in sprint planning and reviews

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Developers**: Collaborate on bug resolution, pair on testing strategy, review pull requests for testability
- **Product Managers**: Review acceptance criteria and validate feature completeness
- **Project Managers**: Provide quality status, flag risks related to testing coverage or timelines
- **UX/UI Designer**: Validate usability aspects during testing

---

## Technical Lead / Architect

### Role Summary
Technical Leads guide technical vision and architecture decisions, ensuring solutions are maintainable, scalable, and aligned with long-term technical strategy. They provide technical mentorship and oversight.

### Responsibilities
- Review and approve technical design documents and architecture decisions
- Provide mentoring and guidance to Developers
- Conduct code reviews with focus on architecture, patterns, and best practices
- Assess technical risks and propose mitigation strategies
- Define technical standards and conventions
- Evaluate new technologies and approaches

### Goals
- Ensure technical quality and architectural consistency
- Minimize technical debt and maintain system scalability
- Build team technical capabilities through mentorship

### Typical Communication
- Architecture decision records (ADRs)
- Technical design reviews and recommendations
- Code review feedback and guidance
- Technical risk assessments

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Developers**: Provide guidance, conduct code reviews, pair on complex technical challenges
- **Project Managers**: Collaborate on technical risk assessment, timeline feasibility, and resource planning
- **Product Managers**: Advise on technical constraints and opportunities, help prioritize technical work
- **Business Analyst**: Ensure technical solutions align with business requirements

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analysts capture and clarify business requirements, ensuring solutions align with business objectives and deliver measurable value. They bridge communication between business stakeholders and technical teams.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate conversations to refine and detail backlog items
- Document clear acceptance criteria for features
- Analyze business processes and identify improvement opportunities
- Validate that solutions meet business needs
- Communicate business impact and ROI of features

### Goals
- Ensure solutions deliver intended business value
- Improve clarity and reduce ambiguity in requirements
- Align technical delivery with business strategy

### Typical Communication
- Requirements documents and user stories
- Business process diagrams and workflows
- Impact analyses and business cases
- Facilitation of requirements workshops

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Product Managers**: Partner on roadmap planning, help translate business needs into product features
- **Developers**: Clarify requirements, provide context for business logic, validate implementation approaches
- **Project Managers**: Support estimation and planning with detailed requirements analysis
- **Support/Customer Success**: Gather insights from customer feedback to inform requirements

---

## Support / Customer Success Lead

### Role Summary
Support and Customer Success Leads champion the customer post-release, managing user feedback, escalations, and adoption. They close the loop between end users and the product team, ensuring customer satisfaction and success.

### Responsibilities
- Gather and report recurring issues, bugs, and feature requests from customers
- Guide users through product adoption, onboarding, and troubleshooting
- Manage customer escalations and coordinate resolution
- Provide customer insights to inform product decisions
- Monitor customer health and satisfaction metrics
- Create and maintain customer-facing documentation and resources

### Goals
- Maximize customer satisfaction and product adoption
- Reduce time to resolution for customer issues
- Provide actionable customer feedback to product teams

### Typical Communication
- Customer health reports and success metrics
- Escalation summaries and resolution tracking
- Customer feedback compilations
- Feature adoption reports

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Product Managers**: Share customer feedback, advocate for customer needs in prioritization discussions
- **QA/Testing**: Coordinate during incident response, provide real-world usage insights for testing scenarios
- **Developers**: Facilitate bug reproduction, provide customer context for technical decisions
- **Project Managers**: Alert to customer-impacting issues, participate in release planning
